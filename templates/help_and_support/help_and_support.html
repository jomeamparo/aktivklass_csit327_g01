Help & Support

How to Join a Class?

To join a class, click on the "Join Class" button in the sidebar. You will need the class code provided by your instructor.

How to Reset Your Password?

If you forget your password, go to the "Settings" section, click on "Reset Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How to Contact Support?

If you need help, please contact support via the "Help & Support" page or email us directly at support@aktivklass.com.

How to Update My Profile?

To update your profile, go to the "Settings" section and click on "Edit Profile".

How to Change My Password?

To change your password, go to "Settings" and click on "Change Password".

How to Add a New Class?

To add a new class, go to the "Faculty" section and click on "Add New Class".

How to Assign Homework?

To assign homework, go to the "Class Record" section and click on "Assign Homework".

How to Submit Homework?

To submit homework, go to the "Class" section, select the class, and click on "Submit Homework".

How to View Class Schedule?

To view your class schedule, go to the "Classes" section and select "View Schedule".

How to Join a Study Group?

To join a study group, go to the "Study Groups" section and select the group you'd like to join.

How to Check Grades?

To check your grades, go to the "Class Record" section and select "View Grades".

How to Contact a Professor?

To contact a professor, go to the "Faculty" section, select the professor, and click on "Contact".
